Cranberry Spinach Salad

Description

This is a simple salad that can be made in minutes. It works well at Thanksgiving or anytime you're just looking for something a little different.

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 ounce) package fresh spinach
  • 1/3 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/3 cup raspberry walnut vinaigrette
  • 1 tablespoon finely shredded Romano cheese

Directions

  1. Combine the spinach, cranberries, walnuts, vinaigrette, and Romano cheese in a large bowl; toss until spinach is evenly coated. Serve immediately.
